# Bilillee - Product Idea & Vision

## Elevator Pitch

Bilillee is the first **"flywheel" marketplace** for beauty and wellness that breaks the traditional SaaS model. Instead of charging monthly subscriptions, we provide premium business management tools **completely free** and only monetize when we deliver real value—new clients. Our 20% first-booking-only commission aligns our success with our customers' growth.

## The Problem

### For Beauty & Wellness Businesses:
1. **High SaaS costs**: Existing solutions charge $100-300/month regardless of business size or success
2. **Fragmented tools**: Scheduling, payments, CRM, and marketing exist in separate silos
3. **Client acquisition is hard**: Marketplaces like ClassPass take 30-50% on EVERY booking, not just new clients
4. **No-shows hurt**: Without deposits or waitlists, empty chairs mean lost revenue

### For Consumers:
1. **Discovery is broken**: Google Maps shows businesses but not real-time availability
2. **Booking friction**: Phone calls, DMs, and back-and-forth messaging
3. **No loyalty tracking**: Paper punch cards get lost, digital solutions require separate apps

## The Solution

**The magic**: Free tools attract businesses → Businesses list on marketplace → Consumers find and book → We earn 20% on first booking only → Revenue funds better free tools → More businesses join.

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

**1. Commission on First Bookings (Primary)**
- 20% of first booking value when client comes via marketplace
- 0% on all subsequent bookings
- Average first booking value: $85
- Commission per new client: ~$17

**2. Subscription Tiers (Future)**
- **Free**: Core tools, up to 2 team members
- **Growth** ($49/mo): Up to 10 team, advanced analytics
- **Pro** ($149/mo): Unlimited team, API access, white-label

**3. Featured Listings**
- Promoted placement in marketplace search
- $99-299/month based on market size

**4. Financial Products (Future)**
- Business loans against future bookings
- Instant payouts (1% fee for same-day)
- Equipment financing

### Unit Economics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**:
- Organic (SEO/content): $0
- Paid social: $150-200 per business
- Referral program: $100 credit

**Lifetime Value (LTV)**:
- Average business: 50 appointments/month
- New clients via marketplace: 5/month (10%)
- Commission revenue: $85/month
- LTV (24-month retention): $2,040

**LTV:CAC Ratio**: 10:1 (excellent)

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Supply-Side (Months 1-6)
**Goal**: 1,000 businesses in launch markets

**Tactics**:
- Direct outreach to top-rated Yelp businesses
- Instagram DM campaigns to stylists with 10k+ followers
- Partnership with beauty schools (graduate placement)
- Free migration from competitors (Square, Mindbody)

**Incentives**:
- $0 commission for first 3 months
- Free branded Instagram booking buttons
- Personal onboarding call

### Phase 2: Demand-Side (Months 4-9)
**Goal**: 10,000 monthly marketplace bookings

**Tactics**:
- Local SEO for "[service] near me"
- Instagram/Facebook ads targeting beauty enthusiasts
- Influencer partnerships (micro-influencers 10k-100k)
- Google Local Services integration

**Features**:
- "New client" discounts promoted in marketplace
- Referral bonuses ($20 credit for referrer and referee)

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 10-18)
**Goal**: 100,000 monthly bookings, expansion to 10 cities

**Tactics**:
- Franchise partnerships (Drybar, European Wax Center)
- Enterprise sales for chains
- International expansion
- Developer platform/API launch

## Risk Analysis

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Low marketplace adoption | Medium | High | Heavy B2B incentives first, guarantee 5 new clients/month |
| Commission model confusion | High | Medium | Clear messaging: "First booking only" |
| Competitor price war | Medium | Medium | Differentiation on features, not just price |
| Payment fraud | Medium | High | Screenshot verification workflow, hold periods |
| Supply concentration | Low | High | Multi-city launch, no single market >30% |

## Success Metrics

### North Star Metric
**Monthly Commission Revenue** = (New clients via marketplace) × (Avg first booking value) × 20%

### Key Performance Indicators

**Business Health**:
- Monthly Active Businesses (MAB)
- Average appointments per business
- Retention rate (6-month, 12-month)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)

**Marketplace Health**:
- Monthly Active Clients (MAC)
- Booking conversion rate
- Repeat booking rate (target: 60%+)
- Average time to book (target: <2 minutes)

**Financial Health**:
- Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)
- Take rate (commission % of GMV)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Payback period
